Projects

Teams of students in CS147 participate in a quarter-long project to design and build a prototype around a set of needs. Each team is comprised of an interdisciplinary group of students with complementing strengths in design and development. The teams are tasked with deliverables throughout the quarter to move them towards a functional proof-of-concept that is presented in slides, posters, and demos at the Industry Project Fair.
