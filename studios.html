Projects

Teams of in CS194H students participate in a quarter-long project that continues an existing prototype started in our introductory HCI course (CS147). Each team is comprised of an interdisciplinary group of upperclassmen with strengths in design and development. The teams are tasked with deliverables throughout the quarter to move them towards a finished, polished product ready for the real world.

Design by Sherman Leung. Last updated 2/2016
